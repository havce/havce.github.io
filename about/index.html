Who we are

2021-08-18 [Updated: 2021-08-18]

We are a group of people who like to break stuff, learn about cybersecurity and enjoy anolinis with Parmigiano Reggiano. We are also the official CTF team of the University of Parma, you can find us on CTFTime. havce was created in 2021, after the 2021 edition of CyberChallenge.IT.
